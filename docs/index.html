Rural Health Signal Monitor

Daily AI-generated summaries of policy and payment news affecting rural hospitals, clinics, and GME pipelines.

This site automatically:

  • Pulls rural-relevant health policy news from RSS feeds (CMS, KFF, Rural Health Information Hub, etc.).
  • Uses an AI model (gpt-4o-mini) to summarize key signals.
  • Publishes a daily digest highlighting risks and opportunities for rural providers and training programs.
